Vishwacon Conference 2023


This conference seeks to unite leading industry experts, academic professionals, researchers, and scholars on a shared platform to exchange knowledge, ideas, and experiences, focusing on the intersection of Millets Awareness, Women's Health for the International Year of Millets. The central theme focuses on a transformative vision for the future, leveraging innovative ideas and discoveries in AI, Communication, IoT, Data Engineering, and Security to positively impact women's well-being.

Publication of Accepted Papers

All accepted & presented papers will be included in Conference Proceedings which will be Published by Taylor and Francis and submitted to Scopus for Inclusion. Selected Papers will be published as Book Chapter in the Edited Book by CRC Press, Taylor and Francis and submitted to Scopus for Inclusion.


Guidelines for Authors

Please ensure your submission meets the conference's strict guidelines for accepting scholarly papers. Downloadable versions of the checklist for full-Text Papers along with templates are available on the conference website.
